Purpose

The Angie's List Grand Prix of Indianapolis web application was designed to provide information about the VIP Grand Prize contest and promote the Grand Prix event.

Requirements Gathering

I met with the project managers to discuss business requirements and brainstorm the design/features.

Design & Development

I created mockups to visualize the design and gain feedback from the digital team. After a couple of design iterations and approval from the team, I worked with the developer to build the landing page using HTML5, CSS3, and AngularJS.
