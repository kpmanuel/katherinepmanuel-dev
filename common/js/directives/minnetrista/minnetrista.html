Purpose

The purpose of this project was to re-design the website for Minnetrista, a museum and recreational center.

Requirements Gathering

I met with the project managers and client to discuss the business requirements and brainstorm the design/features. The current issues involved a problematic navigation and non-responsive user interface. I also inspected the analytics to give me an overview of their website visitors and website behaviors.

Design

We focused on creating more aesthetically unique pages, consistent navigation, and a responsive user interface. I created wireframes and mockups to visualize the design and gain feedback from the client and digital team.

Development

After a couple of design iterations and approval from the client, the development was assigned to an external agency to build the website using a content management system (CMS).
