Purpose

This web application was created to promote the 2-day Fun Pass for Kentucky Kingdom.

Requirements Gathering

I met with the project managers to discuss business requirements and brainstorm the design/features. I also worked with the marketing team to gather the graphical assets to use on the site.

Design & Development

I created mockups to visualize the design and gain feedback from the digital team. After a couple of design iterations and approval from the team, I worked with the developer to build the microsite using HTML5, CSS3, and AngularJS. We finished the fully functional site in about 6 days. This includes re-skinning/re-writing the content for a sister newspaper to provide the same promotion for their market.

Awards

The developer and I won an MVP award for performance and dedication.
